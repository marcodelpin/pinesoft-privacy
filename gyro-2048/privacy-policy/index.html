Privacy Policy — Gyro 2048

Last updated: March 26, 2026

Gyro 2048 is a mobile application developed by PineSoft. This privacy policy describes how we collect, use, and share information when you use Gyro 2048.

What Gyro 2048 Does

Gyro 2048 is a tilt-controlled puzzle game. You merge numbered tiles by tilting your phone, combining them to reach 2048 and beyond.

Sensor Access

Gyro 2048 uses your device's gyroscope and accelerometer sensors for tilt-based gameplay controls:

  • Sensor data is processed entirely on your device in real-time
  • No sensor data is ever recorded, stored, or transmitted to external servers
  • When you close the app, sensor access stops immediately

Information We Collect

Gyro 2048 uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect:

  • Advertising ID: A unique, anonymous identifier used to serve relevant ads
  • Device information: Device model, OS version, and screen size for ad formatting
  • Usage data: Ad interaction data (views, clicks) for analytics

We do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number. Game scores and preferences are stored locally on your device only.

Your Choices

  • Ad personalization: You can opt out of personalized ads through your device settings (Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization).
  • Consent: On first launch, you will be asked for consent to data collection for advertising purposes (EEA/UK users).

Children's Privacy

Gyro 2048 is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Data Retention

We do not store any personal data on our servers. All game data (scores, preferences) is stored locally on your device and can be deleted by uninstalling the app.

GDPR Rights (EEA/UK Users)

If you are in the European Economic Area or United Kingdom, you have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Object to data processing
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority
