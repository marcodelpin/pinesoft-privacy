Privacy Policy — SoundScope

Last updated: March 25, 2026

SoundScope is a mobile application developed by PineSoft. This privacy policy describes how we handle your data when you use SoundScope.

What SoundScope Does

SoundScope is a real-time audio spectrogram and sound recognition app. It uses your device's microphone to visualize audio frequencies and identify sounds using on-device AI.

Microphone Access

SoundScope requires microphone access to function. All audio is processed entirely on your device:

  • Audio is captured, analyzed, and displayed in real-time
  • Sound classification (YAMNet, 521 classes) runs locally via TensorFlow Lite
  • No audio data is ever recorded, stored, or transmitted to external servers
  • When you close the app, audio capture stops immediately

Saved Sound Samples

When you save a sound sample, the audio snippet is stored locally on your device. Saved samples are never uploaded or shared unless you explicitly choose to share them using your device's share functionality.

Advertising

SoundScope displays advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect:

  • Advertising ID: A unique, anonymous identifier used to serve relevant ads
  • Device information: Device model, OS version, and screen size for ad formatting
  • Usage data: Ad interaction data (views, clicks) for analytics

We do not serve adult or inappropriate advertisements. All ads are family-friendly.

Your Choices

  • Microphone: You can revoke microphone permission at any time through your device settings. SoundScope will not function without microphone access.
  • Ad personalization: You can opt out of personalized ads through your device settings (Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization).
  • Consent: On first launch, you will be asked for consent to data collection for advertising purposes (EEA/UK users).

Children's Privacy

SoundScope is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Data Retention

We do not store any personal data on our servers. All app data (preferences, saved samples) is stored locally on your device and can be deleted by uninstalling the app.

GDPR Rights (EEA/UK Users)

If you are in the European Economic Area or United Kingdom, you have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Object to data processing
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority
