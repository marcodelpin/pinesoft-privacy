Privacy Policy — SoundScope

Last updated: March 28, 2026

SoundScope is a mobile application developed by PineSoft (Marco Del Pin). This privacy policy describes how we collect, use, and share information when you use SoundScope.

Information We Collect

SoundScope uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect:

  • Advertising ID: A unique, anonymous identifier used to serve relevant ads.
  • Device information: Device model, operating system version, and screen size for ad formatting.
  • Usage data: Ad interaction data (views, clicks) for analytics.

Microphone Access

SoundScope uses your device's microphone to capture audio for real-time spectrogram visualization and AI-powered sound recognition. Audio data is processed entirely on your device and is never transmitted or shared with any third party.

SoundScope includes an optional recording feature that allows you to save audio samples locally on your device. Saved recordings are stored only on your device and are never uploaded, transmitted, or shared. You can delete saved recordings at any time from within the app or by uninstalling the app.

We do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number. App preferences are stored locally on your device only.

How We Use Information

Information collected through AdMob is used solely for:

  • Displaying advertisements within SoundScope
  • Measuring ad performance and analytics

Third-Party Services

SoundScope uses the following third-party services that may collect data:

  • Google AdMob: Google Privacy Policy

Your Choices

  • Microphone: You can revoke microphone permission at any time through your device settings. SoundScope will not function without microphone access.
  • Ad personalization: You can opt out of personalized ads through your device settings (Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization).
  • Consent: On first launch, you will be asked for consent to data collection for advertising purposes (EEA/UK users).

Children's Privacy

SoundScope is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Data Retention

We do not store any personal data on our servers. All app data (preferences, saved samples) is stored locally on your device and can be deleted by uninstalling the app.

GDPR Rights (EEA/UK Users)

If you are in the European Economic Area or United Kingdom, you have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Object to data processing
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at: support@pinesoft.dev
