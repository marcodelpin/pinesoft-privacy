Privacy Policy

PineSoft Mobile Applications

Effective Date: January 8, 2025

Last Updated: January 8, 2025

1. Introduction

PineSoft ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our mobile applications and games, including PineSoft 2048.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

Our games are designed to work completely offline. We do not require you to create accounts or provide personal information to play our games.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

Our current games (including PineSoft 2048) do not collect any personal data or analytics. All game data (scores, progress, settings) is stored locally on your device only.

2.3 Future Updates

Future versions of our apps may include optional features that collect minimal data to improve user experience. If implemented, these features will:

  • Be clearly disclosed to users before implementation
  • Require explicit user consent through opt-in mechanisms
  • Be limited to non-personal usage statistics and crash reports
  • Comply with all applicable privacy laws including GDPR and CCPA

3. How We Use Information

Currently, we do not collect or use any personal information. All game functionality operates entirely on your device without any data transmission to external servers.

4. Data Storage and Security

All game data (high scores, settings, progress) is stored locally on your device using your device's secure storage systems provided by the operating system. We do not have access to this data, and it never leaves your device.

5. Third-Party Services

Our current games do not integrate with any third-party services. Future updates may include:

  • Advertising: We may display ads through Google AdMob. When implemented, AdMob's privacy policy will apply to any ad-related data collection.
  • Analytics: We may use privacy-focused analytics services to understand general app usage patterns without collecting personal information.

Any third-party integrations will be clearly disclosed in app updates and require appropriate user consent where legally required.

6. Children's Privacy

Our games are suitable for all ages, including children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children of any age. Our games operate entirely offline without any data collection or transmission.

7. Your Rights and Control

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no personal information to access, modify, or delete from our servers. All game data remains under your complete control on your device and can be removed by uninstalling the app.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes in our games, features, or applicable laws. Material changes will be communicated through:

  • Updates to this webpage
  • In-app notifications in updated versions
  • Email notifications if we have your contact information

9. International Users and Compliance

Our privacy practices comply with:

  • GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation) for EU users
  • CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act) for California residents
  • COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) for users under 13
  • Other applicable privacy regulations worldwide

Since we do not collect personal data, compliance requirements are minimal, but we maintain high privacy standards regardless.
