主要 功能

📖

稀有语言文本

出版活动衰退或停止的语言的礼仪和古典文本。为后代进行数字化保护。

📅

19种历法系统

罗马历、科普特历、莫萨拉布历、埃塞俄比亚历、犹太历、伊斯兰历、学术历、修会历和教区历。

📚

圣经翻译

Vulgata Clementina（拉丁语）、Martini 1781和Diodati 1607。按旧约/新约、类别、书卷和章节浏览。

✎

多文字支持

Latin、Arabic、Hebrew、Syriac、Coptic、Ge'ez、Greek、Gothic、Armenian、Devanagari等。

🔍

全文搜索

在所有已安装文本中即时搜索。查找任何段落、经节或祈祷文。

📦

内容包

模块化安装——仅下载您需要的语言和文本。轻量高效。

🌑

深色主题

为沉思阅读而设计。无论白天黑夜，对眼睛都很友好。

🔒

隐私与离线

无需账户，无追踪，无广告。所有内容在设备上本地处理和存储。

⏳

Liturgy of the Hours

Daily office assembler for Lauds, Vespers, and Compline. Navigate by date, browse the assembled liturgy with proper BMD formatting.

🏆

Source Quality Badges

Each content pack displays its source quality: original text, machine translation, or scholarly edition. Know what you are reading.

Development Roadmap

Horatio is in active development. Some features are complete, others are work in progress.

✓

Continuous reader

Scroll through texts with BMD liturgical formatting, flexa/dagger psalm notation, RTL support.

✓

41 content packs

Bibles in 12 languages, lectionaries in 13, breviaries, common prayers, rare language samples.

✓

19 liturgical calendars

Roman Rite engine with 15 national variants, Ambrosian, Mozarabic (Toledo), and Coptic Catholic rites.

✓

Pack Manager

Install, remove, and download content packs. On-demand delivery via Play Asset Delivery.

✓

Georgian translation

Latin Breviary translated to Georgian with TranslateGemma. Georgian fully functional with Latin fallback for missing translations.

✓

Source quality badges

Pack metadata displays source quality indicators: original text vs machine translation, with language and source attribution.

✎

Liturgy of the Hours

HoursAssembler, Navigator, and Reader infrastructure built. Assembles daily office (Lauds, Vespers, Compline) from breviary packs.

✎

Full localization

UI in 22 languages. BMD section labels translated in 12 languages. Calendar i18n in 22 locales including Georgian, Japanese, Korean, Chinese, Arabic, Coptic, Greek, Hebrew, Syriac.

✎

Text quality validation

Cross-referencing multiple sources to find and fix errors in liturgical texts.
