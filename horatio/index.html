Horatio

Digital preservation of texts in rare, endangered, and classical languages

Many languages are alive in daily prayer and conversation, but their written traditions are disappearing. Horatio preserves these texts in a modern, offline-first reader with liturgical calendar awareness.

PRIVACY POLICY

Horatio is developed by Marco Del Pin (PineSoft).

This app is designed with privacy in mind:

  • No ads — the app contains no advertising
  • No tracking — no analytics, no telemetry, no user profiling
  • No account required — all features work without registration
  • No internet required — all content is stored locally on your device
  • No data collection — we do not collect, store, or share any personal data
  • No third-party services — no Firebase, no Google Analytics, no crash reporting

The app stores content packs, reading progress, and user preferences locally on your device only. This data is never transmitted to any server.

If you choose to use the optional text-to-speech feature, audio is generated locally using your device's built-in TTS engine.

In-app purchases: Optional tip jar (coffee/supporter/patron). No content is locked behind payments — all texts are free.

For questions about this privacy policy, contact: support@pinesoft.dev
