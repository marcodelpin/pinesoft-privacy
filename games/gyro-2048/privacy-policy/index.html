Gyroscope and Accelerometer

Gyro 2048 uses your device's gyroscope and accelerometer sensors for gameplay controls. This motion data is:

  • Processed entirely on your device in real-time
  • Never recorded, stored, or transmitted to any server
  • Used only to determine tile movement direction during gameplay
  • Not captured when the app is in the background

Google Play Games Services

Gyro 2048 optionally integrates with Google Play Games for leaderboards and achievements. If you sign in:

  • Your scores are submitted to Google Play leaderboards
  • Achievement progress is tracked by Google
  • This data is governed by Google's Privacy Policy

You can play without signing in — Google Play Games is entirely optional.

Advertising

Gyro 2048 displays advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may use anonymous device identifiers to serve relevant ads. We do not serve adult or inappropriate advertisements. All ads are family-friendly.

You can review Google AdMob's privacy practices at: Google AdMob Privacy

In-App Purchases

Gyro 2048 may offer optional purchases (e.g., "Remove Ads") through Google Play. These transactions are processed entirely by Google Play and are subject to Google's Privacy Policy. PineSoft does not receive any personal payment information.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transmit any personal data to third parties. Game data and scores remain on your device unless you opt into Google Play Games.

Data Retention

Game progress and scores are stored locally on your device. Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data.

Children's Privacy

Gyro 2048 is designed to be family-friendly and suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app complies with COPPA requirements.

Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

Gyro 2048 does not collect personal data on external servers. The only data processed externally is anonymous advertising identifiers by Google AdMob and optional leaderboard scores via Google Play Games.

Under GDPR (EEA/UK) and CCPA (California), you have the right to:

  • Access, rectify, or delete any personal data
  • Opt out of personalized advertising (via device settings)
  • Disconnect Google Play Games at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@pinesoft.dev.
