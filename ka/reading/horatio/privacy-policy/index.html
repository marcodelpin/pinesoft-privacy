Privacy Policy

App: Horatio
Developer: PineSoft
Last updated: March 2026

Horatio is developed by PineSoft. This privacy policy explains how Horatio handles your data.

How Your Data Is Used

Horatio processes all data locally on your device. No data is transmitted to external servers. The app works completely offline after initial content pack downloads.

  • Reading preferences (font size, theme, selected calendar) are stored in local app storage and never transmitted.
  • Content packs (.htx files) are stored locally in the app's private directory.
  • Full-text search uses a local SQLite FTS5 index on your device.

Third-Party Services

Horatio does not use any third-party services that collect data:

  • No Google AdMob
  • No Firebase Analytics or Crashlytics
  • No third-party tracking or advertising SDKs

Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transmit any personal data to third parties. All data remains on your device.

Data Retention

All data is stored locally on your device. Uninstalling the app removes all stored data, including preferences and downloaded content packs.

Children's Privacy

Horatio is suitable for all ages. We do not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. The app complies with COPPA requirements.

Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

Since Horatio does not collect or store any personal data on external servers, there is no personal data to access, rectify, delete, or port. All data is under your full control on your device.

If you have questions about your data rights, contact us at the email below.

In-App Purchases

Horatio may offer optional tip jar purchases through your device's app store (Google Play or Apple App Store). These transactions are processed entirely by the respective platform and are subject to their privacy policies (Google, Apple). PineSoft does not receive any personal payment information.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.
