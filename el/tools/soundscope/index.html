Key Features

🎶

AI Sound Recognition

Powered by YAMNet, identifies 521 sound classes in real-time — birds, instruments, traffic, appliances and more.

🎨

Spectrogram Art

Turn images into sound! Play shapes that appear as art on another phone's spectrogram. 9 built-in presets, or upload your own image with Pro.

🌈

Scientific Colormaps

5 perceptually uniform colormaps: Inferno, Viridis, Magma, Plasma, and Classic.

📡

Full Spectrum

20 Hz to 20 kHz logarithmic spectrogram with adaptive frequency labels. Blackman-Harris windowing for -92 dB sidelobe suppression.

📍

GPS Sound Recording

Record outdoor sound walks with GPS coordinates. View detected sounds plotted on a map along your route. Pro feature.

🔇

Noise Suppression

Adaptive per-bin spectral subtraction removes background noise, revealing what matters.

🎧

Frequency Presets

Quick-switch between Birding, Speech, Music, and Full Range modes to focus on what you're listening for.

🌐

9 Languages

Full interface and all 521 detection labels in EN, IT, DE, FR, ES, PT, JA, KO, ZH.

⚫

Audio Recording

Record audio as WAV files with one tap. Recordings are saved locally on your device — never uploaded or shared.

📱

Home Screen Widget

See the last detected sound at a glance without opening the app.

Spectrogram Art

Play a sound file and watch hidden images appear on your friend's spectrogram. A fun way to share visual messages through sound.
