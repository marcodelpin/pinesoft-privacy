Privacy Policy

App: Gyro 15 Puzzle
Developer: PineSoft
Last updated: March 2026

Gyroscope and Accelerometer

Gyro 15 Puzzle uses your device's gyroscope and accelerometer sensors for gameplay controls. This motion data is:

  • Processed entirely on your device in real-time
  • Never recorded, stored, or transmitted to any server
  • Used only to determine tile movement direction during gameplay
  • Not captured when the app is in the background

Leaderboards and Achievements

Gyro 15 Puzzle optionally integrates with platform game services (Google Play Games on Android, Game Center on iOS) for leaderboards and achievements. If you sign in:

  • Your scores are submitted to platform leaderboards
  • Achievement progress is tracked by the platform
  • This data is governed by the platform's privacy policy (Google, Apple)

You can play without signing in — leaderboards and achievements are entirely optional.

Advertising

Gyro 15 Puzzle displays advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may use anonymous device identifiers to serve relevant ads. We do not serve adult or inappropriate advertisements. All ads are family-friendly.

You can review Google AdMob's privacy practices at: Google AdMob Privacy

In-App Purchases

Gyro 15 Puzzle may offer optional purchases (e.g., "Remove Ads") through your device's app store (Google Play or Apple App Store). These transactions are processed entirely by the respective platform and are subject to their privacy policies (Google, Apple). PineSoft does not receive any personal payment information.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transmit any personal data to third parties. Game data and scores remain on your device unless you opt into platform game services (leaderboards).

Data Retention

Game progress and scores are stored locally on your device. Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data.

Children's Privacy

Gyro 15 Puzzle is designed to be family-friendly and suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app complies with COPPA requirements.

Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

Gyro 15 Puzzle does not collect personal data on external servers. The only data processed externally is anonymous advertising identifiers by Google AdMob and optional leaderboard scores via platform game services.

Under GDPR (EEA/UK) and CCPA (California), you have the right to:

  • Access, rectify, or delete any personal data
  • Opt out of personalized advertising (via device settings)
  • Disconnect platform game services at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@pinesoft.dev.
